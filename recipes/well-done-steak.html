Well-Done Steak

Description

A well-done steak is a steak that has been thoroughly cooked, with little-to-no pink or red in the middle.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt and/or pepper
  • Butter or oil
  • Garlic, thyme or rosemary (optional)

Steps

  1. Season
  2. Heat
  3. Sear
  4. Check for doneness
  5. Rest
  6. Serve
